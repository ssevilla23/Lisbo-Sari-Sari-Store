Our History

The store's history began in the early 20th century as a tiny Sari-Sari store. Our mother would travel to the mainland and became acquainted with someone who owned a grocery store. She then began to (Alsada) the products and sell them back home to make a modest profit. This store is where we grew up alongside our mother as she balanced being a full-time mom, working outside the home in farming jobs, and managing the Sari-Sari store.

Over the years, the store was often on and off due to financial struggles until all her children grew up and moved to different places to find better opportunities. Our mother decided to close the Sari-Sari store temporarily to help her children in the city by doing household chores, giving them more time to study while working. Once all her children had their own families and lives, she decided to reopen the store with their help. Her perseverance, determination, and hard work built the Lisbo Sari-Sari store we have today!
